📋 How the Real Test Works

  • Tell Me Question: You'll be asked 1 random "Tell Me" question at the start of the test. You must answer from memory.
  • Show Me Question: During driving, the examiner will ask 1 random "Show Me" question. You demonstrate the control.
  • Scoring: Each correct answer is 1 point. You can get 0, 1, or 2 points total.
  • Pass Requirement: You won't fail the test for getting these wrong, but they count as minor faults.
